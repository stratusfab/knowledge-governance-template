# AI Model Guide

## Copilot
Best for repo-aware reasoning, multi-file context, governance enforcement, and MCP orchestration.

## Claude
Best for long-context synthesis, comparing large documents, extracting claims from PDFs, and conflict analysis.

## GPT Models
Best for creative generation, rewriting, narrative structure, and proposal drafting.

## Local Models
Best for privacy-sensitive workflows, batch processing, and offline ingestion.
